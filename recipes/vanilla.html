Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Vanilla ice cream is a classic and universally loved frozen dessert known for its smooth and creamy texture, along with its delicate and sweet flavor derived from vanilla Vanilla ice cream has a luscious and velvety texture that melts in the mouth. It is characterized by a perfect balance between firmness and creaminess, creating a delightful experience with every spoonful. The primary flavor of vanilla ice cream comes from the use of vanilla extract or vanilla bean. The taste is sweet, with subtle notes of the rich, aromatic vanilla bean. Follow along my journey of making these wonderful dessert for your family and friends and enjoy hot summer days with your beloved ones.

Ingredients

  1. 2 quarts half-and-half
  2. half cups white sugar
  3. 1/2 pint heavy cream
  4. 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  5. 1 pinch Salt

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and whole milk until well combined.
  2. Add granulated sugar and continue to whisk until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. The salt enhances the flavor and helps with the freezing process.
  4. Once the mixture is thoroughly chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker.
  5. Freeze the ice cream for at least 4-6 hours or until it reaches a firm consistency. You can also leave it overnight for best results
  6. Scoop the vanilla ice cream into bowls or cones
  7. Garnish with your favorite toppings like chocolate sauce, nuts, or fresh fruit, if desired.
  8. Enjoy with your friends and kids!
